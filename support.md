# Vossi — Support

Need help with Vossi? We're happy to assist.

## Contact

The best way to reach us is to **open an issue** on our GitHub page:

👉 [open an issue](https://github.com/pietervdw87/vossi-support/issues)

Please describe your question or problem and, if helpful, your device and iOS version. We aim to respond within a few business days.

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I change the language?**
Open **Settings** (the gear icon). You can choose the app language and, separately, the language you want to learn.

**How do I hear a word pronounced?**
Make sure your device sound is on. Tap the speaker icon on a word in the album, or tap a word card while playing.

**How do I turn on daily reminders?**
Go to **Settings → Reminders**. If notifications are turned off for Vossi in your iOS Settings, the app will point you there.

**What is the streak?**
Your streak counts the days in a row you play. Play every day to keep it alive — a streak freeze can cover one missed day.

**How do I dress up my fox?**
Open the **Fox** tab. Earn hats, glasses and more by learning words, completing themes, and keeping your streak going. Tap an unlocked item to wear it.

**Is my progress saved?**
Yes — your progress is stored on your device.

**Does Vossi collect my data?**
No. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md).
